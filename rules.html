--- layout: article id: rules title: Rules ---
  • Parent or guardian required for PK-5
  • To ensure a consistent pace, we recommend independent riders be on pedal bikes.
  • All kid-carrying bikes are welcome!
  • Rule for kids:
    • All riders must stay behind the designated leader. Note that marshalls will ride ahead to block intersections.
    • All riders must stay in front of the designated caboose.
    • All riders must stay to the right of the median.
